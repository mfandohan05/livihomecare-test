Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 21, 2025

At Livi Home Care, we respect your privacy and are committed to protecting the information you share with us through your use of this website. This Privacy Policy explains what data we collect, how we use it, and your rights regarding your personal information.

1. Information We Collect

We use Google Analytics to gather anonymous data about how visitors use our website. This includes information such as:

  • Pages visited and time spent on each page
  • Browser type, operating system, and screen resolution
  • Device type (mobile, tablet, desktop)
  • Approximate geographic location (city, country)
  • Referring websites or links

This data is collected via cookies and similar technologies to help us improve our website and better understand visitor behavior.

2. Use of Cookies

We use cookies for basic website functionality and analytics purposes. Cookies are small data files stored on your device that help us recognize repeat visitors and track usage patterns.

You may choose to accept or reject non-essential cookies through our cookie consent banner. If you reject non-essential cookies, Google Analytics will be disabled and will not collect data during your session.

3. Google Analytics

We use Google Analytics, a service provided by Google LLC, to understand how users interact with our site. Google Analytics uses cookies to collect information anonymously. This information does not personally identify you and is used solely for statistical reporting purposes.

For more information on how Google Analytics collects and processes data, please visit:
https://policies.google.com/technologies/partner-sites

You can opt out of Google Analytics by installing the browser add-on available at:
https://tools.google.com/dlpage/gaoptout

4. Do We Sell Your Information?

No. Livi Home Care does not sell, rent, or trade your personal information to any third parties.

5. Your Rights (CCPA & U.S. State Laws)

If you are a California resident, you may have the right to:

  • Request access to the personal data we may have about you
  • Request deletion of your personal information
  • Opt out of the sale or sharing of your data (we do not sell data)
